About PrivaLex Advisory Welcome to PrivaLex Advisory, where strategy meets robust compliance.

PrivaLex Advisory is a global data protection and privacy consultancy firm that provides outsourced Data Protection Officer (DPO) services, EU and UK Representative services, Cyber Security Consultancy, Tech Policy Research and Advisory, Data Protection Academy, and AI Governance and Compliance services. We are a team of experienced data protection and privacy professionals who are passionate about helping businesses navigate the complex data protection and privacy landscape. Our mission is to help businesses achieve robust data protection and privacy compliance while enabling them to leverage data to drive innovation and growth.
